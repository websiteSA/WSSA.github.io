About Us

Sudha Automobile is a dynamic engineering mechanical and Electrical lighting service company specializing in inspection, maintenance, repair, and supply solutions for heavy earth moving machinery (HEMM) and mobile equipment. Established in 2021, the company is driven by industry expertise and a commitment to operational excellence, reliability, and safety.

Founded by a former professional from Tata Steel, Sudha Automobile brings deep technical knowledge of equipment systems, design, and maintenance practices, enabling high-quality service delivery across diverse industrial applications.

Core Business Areas

1. Maintenance & Engineering Services

  • Inspection, maintenance, and repair of mobile equipment and mobile cranes
  • Health assessment and safety system improvement
  • Annual Rate Contracts (ARC) for complete maintenance
  • Preventive, predictive (TBM & CBM), and breakdown maintenance
